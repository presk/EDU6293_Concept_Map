Self-regulation

“Developing students' self-regulation skills is of great importance in education, as a key aim of education is to help them develop the capacity to guide and improve their own learning” (Xiao & Yang, 2019). Self-regulation is about giving students the tools to learn by themselves.

Xiao, & Yang, M. (2019). Formative assessment and self-regulated learning: How formative assessment supports students’ self-regulation in English language learning. System (Linköping), 81, 39–49. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.system.2019.01.004
