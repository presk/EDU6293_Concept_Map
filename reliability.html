Reliability
In classical test theory it is defined as the level of random errors in a test (Raykov & Macoulides, 2011). It is usually evaluated by using Cronbach’s alpha. In a more general context, it is defined as an assessment’s consistency, the extent to which it measures what it is intended to measure (Andrade & Heritage, 2017).

Raykov, T. & Marcoulides, G.A. (2011). Introduction to Psychometric Theory. New York: Routledge.
