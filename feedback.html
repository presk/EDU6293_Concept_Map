Feedback
Feedback is about giving students comments on how to improve their work. It is important to use feedback as an opportunity to promote self-efficacy and self-regulation. However, it is important to direct feedback towards tasks instead of generic "good jobs" (Andrade & Heritage, 2017).

Andrade, H.L., & Heritage, M. (2017). Using Formative Assessment to Enhance Learning, Achievement, and Academic Self-Regulation (1st ed.). Routledge. https://doi-org.proxy.bib.uottawa.ca/10.4324/9781315623856. Chapter 3.
